me in another universe

Yup, that's me. I was born in 1987 to Renée and Charlie Swan, but my parents split when I was a baby. So it's really was just the two of us, me and my mom , moving around between California and Arizona. I would visit my dad in Forks from time to time, tho it never quite felt like home, at least not at first. I was always a bit of an outsider, never really 'fitting in' no matter where I lived. Always having my nose in books, away from people. I did think about becoming a teacher one day, like my mom. My mom remarried and found happiness with Phil. But he travels a lot for his work. I decided to move to Forks to live with my dad so she could travel with him.
since january 18, 2005
